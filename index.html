About Me

Hi, I'm Prajwal (Praj), a 15‑year‑old tech enthusiast from India. I love coding, making robots, exploring tech and creating youtube content. I started learning to code when I was 10 years (2020) during the covid-19 pandemic, I started learning block coding through MIT app inventor at the age of 10, when I turned 12, I started learning HTML and CSS(That's when I explored the world of real coding). I joined a coding class where I further learnt HTML and CSS. At the age of 14, I learnt Python and JS a little bit.

I started my YouTube channel, Praj Tech. On the channel, I create videos related to different areas of tech, exploring useful ideas, tools, and concepts that people can learn from. My goal is to make content that is interesting, informative, and easy for viewers to understand. Working on Praj Tech has also helped me become more creative and consistent, as I keep trying new ideas and improving my videos over time while growing my audience.
